01 Executive Summary

As of this writing, Vantage IR has approximately 36 hours of active forensic work on Sawatch Health System's environment. We have identified a credible initial access pathway and confirmed the scope of encryption. Several critical questions remain open, including the full extent of data movement and whether attacker access has been fully eliminated.

This memo is intended to give your leadership team a structured picture of what we know, what we are investigating, and where we need decisions from you. It is not a root cause report. Final findings will follow once we have completed log recovery, credential audit, and storage integrity analysis.

04 Critical Open Questions UNRESOLVED

The following questions are unresolved as of this memo. These have a direct bearing on containment confidence, restoration sequencing, and breach determination. We will update as findings develop.

Exfiltration scope Was data successfully exfiltrated externally, or is it staged internally? Volume and target systems remain under analysis. This directly affects breach notification obligations.
Attacker persistence We have not confirmed that all attacker access has been eliminated. Until identity infrastructure is validated, we cannot assert the environment is clean. Restoration before this is confirmed carries risk.
Backup integrity Given the dwell period, the attacker had access to backup systems. We are analyzing whether backup jobs were altered, deleted, or targeted. Some restore points may not be reliable.
Identity infrastructure trust If the attacker had domain controller access during the dwell period, a full restoration of Active Directory trust is required before enterprise systems can be considered clean. This significantly affects recovery timeline estimates.
Insider allegation status Current evidence is more consistent with compromised credentials than deliberate insider action. We are continuing to review. The employee should not be treated as confirmed involved based on current data alone.
Full dwell period duration We have a minimum estimate. We do not yet have a confirmed start date for attacker access. This affects scope of exposure and notification obligations.
05 Requests to Sawatch Leadership ACTION REQUIRED
  • Do not restore systems to production from recent backup copies without our sign-off. We need to validate the integrity of restore points before they are used. Recent copies may contain attacker artifacts or corrupted states.
  • Hold off on full credential resets until we have confirmed the AD scope. Partial resets in an environment where the attacker may still have persistence can alert the actor before containment is complete.
  • Preserve all VPN appliance logs, session records, and firewall logs in their current state. Do not permit patching, firmware updates, or configuration changes to affected network appliances until we have extracted forensic artifacts.
  • Confirm whether out-of-band communications channels are in use for incident coordination. If leadership is coordinating this incident through email or collaboration tools on the same identity infrastructure, we should discuss whether that channel is trustworthy.
